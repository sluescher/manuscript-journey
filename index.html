Birth of a Book, 1485

In the flickering lamplight of al-Azhar's scriptorium, a scribe carefully forms each letter of what will become manuscript Cairo, Dar al-Kutub 123. This book of Islamic jurisprudence will witness the greatest political transformation in Cairo's medieval history.

Source: Colophon data from Cairo, National Library MS 123

Tremors of Change, 1516

News arrives that Sultan Selim has defeated the Safavids at Chaldiran. Egyptian scholars begin to worry about their future under potential Ottoman rule.
